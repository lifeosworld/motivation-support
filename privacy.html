2. Information We Collect

At this time, Future Me does not require user registration and does not collect personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number

  • Government-issued identification

The app also does not request access to your contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or location.

3. Permissions We Request

Future Me may ask for permission to send local notifications (for example, scheduled motivational reminders you configure yourself). This permission is entirely optional and can be turned on or off at any time from within the app or your device's Settings. Notifications are scheduled and delivered directly on your device — no data is transmitted to us or any third party as part of this feature.

4. Technical Information

To maintain the performance and reliability of the app, standard technical information may be collected automatically by Apple or the operating system, such as:

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • App crash reports
  • Diagnostic information

This information is used solely to improve the stability, security, and performance of the application and does not identify you personally.

5. How Information Is Used

Any technical or diagnostic information available through Apple services is used exclusively to:

  • Improve app performance
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Enhance the user experience

6. Third-Party Services

Future Me does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Your use of the app is not shared with external companies for marketing or advertising purposes.

7. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

8. Data Storage

Because Future Me does not collect personal information, no personally identifiable user data is stored on our servers.

9. Your Choices and Data Deletion

App preferences you set — such as your display name, selected topics, and favorite phrases — are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted to our servers. Since there is no account and no server-side copy of this information, you can remove it at any time simply by deleting the app from your device.

10. Children's Privacy

Future Me is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13, in accordance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete it.
